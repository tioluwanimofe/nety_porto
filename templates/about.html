About me

About Tioluwanimofe Adesanya… a.k.a The Choleric Sanguine

Tioluwanimofe Adesanya, a software engineer, content creator and editor all by profession is a high school graduate from Landmark University Secondary School. He is from a family of five comprising two siblings, a mom and his dad. This young entrepreneur has edited many articles with some including, The Four Personalities of an individual, Smart Talks with Mofe, and Amy’s dream all of which have been recognized on various cross editing platforms.

His Career

Growing up in the mega city of Lagos State, Tioluwanimofe like every other entrepreneur began struggling to make a name for himself by taking content creation at the age of fourteen. In which he marketed and showcased his natural talents by working under his mom, the CEO of Reaps ventures. At the age of sixteen he once again challenged his entrepreneurial skills by delving into the world of information Technology with the aspirations of becoming the world’s most remarkable software engineer with an African background. Up till this date, Tioluwanimofe has taken this dream of his by the neck, seizing every moment to develop himself as well as his community.

His Education

He began his education at the age of two years from receiving his first two years of primary education at Oakdale infant mixed school, he advanced to continue this first step into education at Abbey nursery and primary school, at which he attained many awards and recognitions at a very young age. On moving to his next steps, Tioluwanimofe, attended Landmark University Secondary School at which he attained his High School Diploma.

His Hobbies

Being a Choleric Sanguine by personality, Tioluwanimofe, has been using this special personality to impact those around him, be it by vocally spreading the news of opportunities and scholarships to those around him, or organizing training to shorten the gap between Technological illiteracy in an African home. In a personal interview he once said he always felt his God given calling was to break the Technological barrier in his home community thus earning him the nickname Techno-king.
